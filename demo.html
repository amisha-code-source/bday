How to Use

  1. Click "Launch Website" to open the birthday wishes collection
  2. Fill in your name and birthday wish in the form
  3. Click "Submit Wish" to add it to the collection
  4. View all wishes in the beautiful card layout
  5. Hover over cards to delete them if needed
